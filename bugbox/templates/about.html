Hi, thanks for checking out the Bugbox. This is an issue/bug tracking application.

The site is currently set up to simulate a software development project with five different sub-teams. Users can create issues, communicate with team members and submit issues to Team Leads for review.

By default, all newly registered users are regular users that are not assigned to any sub-team. If you would like to view the app from the perspective of a Site Admin or Team Lead, click on one of the “login with x” links on the login page.

The following are features for each privilege level:

Regular User
  • Create new issues (by default Regular Users assign themselves to any issues created)
  • Comment on assigned issues
  • Submit assigned issues for review
Team Lead
  • Everything a Regular User can do
  • Add/remove their team label from an issue
  • Claim users that do not belong to any sub-team
  • Remove users from their team
  • Assign members of their team to an issue
  • Delete issues with their team label
  • Review submitted issues with their team label
Site Admin
  • Everything a Team Lead can do across all sub-teams
  • Promote a Regular User to Team Lead
  • Demote a Team Lead to Regular User
  • Switch the team of any user
Others
  • Search Issues in searchbar
  • PFP
