Hello, This is an issue/bug tracking application. After registering/loging in with an account you can create and work on issues accross three differenet permissions levels.

Regular User
  • X Create new issues
  • X Comment on issues they are assigned to or created
  • X Submit issues for review
Team Lead
  • X Everything a regular user can
  • X Add or remove their team label from an issue
  • X Claim users with no team as part of their Team
  • X Remove users from their team
  • X Review issues within their team
  • X Assign members of their team to work on an issue
  • X Delete issues in their team
Admin
  • Everything a team lead can do (accross all teams)
  • Promote a user to team Lead
  • Demote a team lead to user
  • Switch the team of any user/team lead
  • delete user (may be annoying withu updating comments and stuff)
